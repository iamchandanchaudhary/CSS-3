Fundamentals of Cyber Law

The fundamentals of cyber law encompass several key areas:

  • Legal Framework for Digital Transactions: Establishing the legality and security of electronic transactions and digital signatures.
  • Data Protection and Privacy: Safeguarding personal data and ensuring privacy in the digital realm.
  • Cybercrime: Defining and penalizing various forms of cybercrimes such as hacking, identity theft, and online fraud.
  • Intellectual Property: Protecting intellectual property rights in the context of digital content and software.
  • Regulation of Digital Platforms: Governing the conduct and responsibilities of intermediaries like social media and e-commerce platforms.
